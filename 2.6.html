1.6 Access Issues

Access issues refers to the issues that a person may have when accessing a website. While this is applicable to most people there are different requirements that must be met for those with specific disabilities. Small text can be difficult to read for people with poor eyesight so making the text on a page large enough for them to read easily without cluttering up the page should be considered when designing a website.

Making buttons and other clickable links larger is helpful to anyone who accesses the website as making the button larger makes it easier to click. Colour blindness can also affect a person's ability to access a website.
